About Us
Sri Vishnu Educational Society has stood first among equals because of its quest and hard work to develop as an educational society which stands apart. Our founder chairman, Late. Padma Bhushan Dr. B.V. Raju, through his excellent vision and foresight established many educational institutions. These institutions are play a vital role in molding and shaping the future of thousands of students year after year.
